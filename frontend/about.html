ABOUT

Veerangana is a women safety web platform dedicated to enabling safer lives for women through real-time emergency reporting, resources, and help connections. Our mission is to stand for the fearless — providing tools to report violence, seek help, and build awareness.

"नारी कोई अबला नहीं, शक्ति है वह, समय आने पर अंगार भी बन सकती है।"

This project reflects the need for strong systems supporting women’s dignity and courage. With Veerangana, we aim to contribute a small but impactful step toward a safer, empowered society for women.
