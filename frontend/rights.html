Women's Rights in India

  • 🛡️ Right to Equality under Article 14 of the Indian Constitution.
  • 👩‍⚖️ Right against discrimination (Article 15).
  • 📚 Right to Education for girls under RTE Act.
  • 👮 Right against domestic violence (Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005).
  • 🗣️ Right to dignity and decency during investigations and arrests.
  • 🔐 Right to privacy in medical procedures (especially for rape survivors).
  • 👩‍⚕️ Right to maternity benefit under the Maternity Benefit Act, 1961.
  • 📞 Right to free legal aid and complaint registration.
